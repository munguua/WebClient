ProtonMail 4.0 is a brand new inbox that contains many new features while improving the privacy and security that Proton is known for.

Use the app switcher to quickly move between Proton apps without having to log back in.

ProtonCalendar is now available in Beta. Because your calendar is encrypted, not even we can see the details of your events.

Our dark mode is finally here! You can use it even if your device settings are not configured for dark mode.

Switch dark mode on and off by going to Settings > Appearance > Themes

Reach us from within ProtonMail 4.0, right from the top bar. Reporting a bug will send your comments directly to our team.
