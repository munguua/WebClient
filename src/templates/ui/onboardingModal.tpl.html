Welcome to ProtonMail 4.0 Meet the app switcher Introducing ProtonCalendar Introducing Dark Mode Have questions or feedback?

ProtonMail 4.0 is a brand new inbox which contains many new features while continuing to improve the privacy and security that Proton is known for.

We would like to walk you through important new features and user experience improvements.

The app switcher allows you to quickly switch between Proton apps while never having to log back in.

ProtonMail, ProtonContacts, ProtonCalendar, and all future Proton services (e.g. ProtonDrive) are just a couple of clicks away.

ProtonCalendar is a new encrypted calendar to go with ProtonMail and it is now available in Beta.

Now you can create events which are encrypted in a way that even we cannot see the details of your events.

Our dark mode is finally here and you can use it regardless of your device’s configuration.

Switch to the dark mode now or navigate at any point in time to Settings > Appearance > Themes

Can’t find something? Want to say hello? We are here to listen to your feedback.

Reach out to us from within ProtonMail v4, right from the top bar. Reporting a bug will send your comments directly to our team.
